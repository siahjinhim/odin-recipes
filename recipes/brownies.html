Brownies

How to bake brownies

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder
  • Flour
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Melt the butter on the stove, then remove from heat and add the remaining wet ingredients.
  2. Beat in the dry ingredients. Spread the batter in the pan and bake.
  3. Stir the frosting ingredients together until well-combined, then frost the brownies while they are still warm. Cut into squares when cool.
